ABOUT US

Five1O is an events and music collective that seeks to serve it's community through sound and entertainment. This collective aims to bring quality talent to the East Bay community while simultaneously discovering and promoting local artists. Providing regular, quality and affordable events to a largely student community are staple to our mission. This years calendar is full of exciting and engaging events including warehouse parties, DJ competitions, block parties and club gigs in both SF & East bay. Follow the page to stay up to date on the latest happening within the Five1O. Five1O was founded by UC Berkeley students who aim to provide arts and entertainment to more than just the student body. So all are welcome! Are you a DJ / Vocalist / Artist or entertainer? Feel free to reach out for upcoming opportunities!
